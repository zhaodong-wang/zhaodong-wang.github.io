# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiting, research, engineering, and product collaborators who need to quickly understand Zhaodong Wang's current senior profile, recent AI-agent work, public research signal, and contact path.

## Product Purpose

This portfolio presents a compact public narrative for a senior engineer and research scientist. It moves the old site away from a full academic archive and toward a current "one paper" profile: agent systems first, public research as proof, education as context.

## Brand Personality

Precise, cinematic, systems-minded.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing pages, inflated product-marketing copy, confidential internal product names or scale metrics, dense CV archives, default AI gradients, and decorative effects that obscure the work.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the current senior-agent-systems profile.
- Use public proof instead of confidential internal numbers, code names, or adoption metrics.
- Preserve the legacy portfolio's dark/gold motion language while modernizing the implementation.
- Keep research compact, selective, and linked to public sources.
- Make animation feel intentional, optional, and accessible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, semantic HTML, keyboard-accessible navigation and links, visible focus states, and reduced-motion alternatives for major animation.
